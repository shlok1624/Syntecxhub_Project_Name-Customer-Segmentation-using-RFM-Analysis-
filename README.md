# 📊 Customer Segmentation using RFM Analysis

### Data Analyst Internship Assignment – Syntecxhub

An interactive **Customer Segmentation Dashboard** built using **Power BI** to analyze customer purchasing behavior through **RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) Analysis**. This project demonstrates data cleaning, customer segmentation, DAX calculations, and interactive dashboard visualization to support data-driven marketing decisions.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

The objective of this project is to transform raw transactional customer data into meaningful business insights using the RFM model.

The dashboard enables users to:

- Analyze customer purchasing behavior.
- Calculate Recency, Frequency, and Monetary metrics.
- Segment customers into meaningful groups.
- Identify loyal, at-risk, and new customers.
- Explore customer distribution using interactive visuals.
- Support targeted marketing strategies through customer segmentation.

## 📈 RFM Metrics

The dashboard calculates the following customer metrics:

- **Recency** – Number of days since the customer's last purchase.
- **Frequency** – Total number of purchases made by the customer.
- **Monetary** – Total amount spent by the customer.

These metrics are used to assign customers into different RFM segments.

---

## 👥 Customer Segments

Customers are classified into groups such as:

- 🏆 Champions
- ❤️ Loyal Customers
- 🌱 Potential Loyalists
- 🆕 New Customers
- ⚠️ At Risk
- ❌ Lost Customers

## 💡 Key Insights

- Loyal customers contribute a significant share of total revenue.
- A considerable number of customers are identified as **At Risk**, indicating opportunities for retention campaigns.
- Champions exhibit the highest purchase frequency and spending.
- New customers require engagement strategies to encourage repeat purchases.
- Customer segmentation helps businesses optimize marketing efforts and improve customer retention.

---

## 📢 Marketing Recommendations

- Reward **Champions** with exclusive offers and loyalty programs.
- Encourage **Potential Loyalists** through personalized promotions.
- Re-engage **At Risk** customers with discounts and reminder campaigns.
- Welcome **New Customers** with onboarding offers and product recommendations.
- Focus retention efforts on high-value customers to maximize long-term profitability.
